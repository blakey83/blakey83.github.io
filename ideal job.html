My ideal job would be the Strategic Technology Lead, Operations Transformation from Woodside Energy Ltd advertised on Seek
on the 3rd of March 2021. This is a leadership position for Woodside, a well know offshore Oil and Gas. This position would
involve coordinating the company’s technology transformation program. There would be a need to manage a number of small teams
delivering individual projects for the overall technology strategy. There would also be a need of informing and managing
relevant stakeholders, as well as the management and quality assurance of technology vendors.

This position appeals to me because I like interacting with people, especially about technology and business. It would be
a challenging role, as you would be responsible for the output of a number of teams, as well as the individuals within those
teams. There would be pressure to ensure that teams perform to high standard. Also I enjoy technology projects, as there is a
lot of satisfaction in watching something that was once only an Idea, become a reality.

For this job, you would need a degree in either computer science of Information Technology. The skill that would be required
would include good people skills, high levels of IT skills and leadership skills. You would also need about 10 to 15 years’
experience in the technology field. Part of this experience would be in a leadership role, as well experience with project delivery.
It would also be advantageous to hold a cloud computing certification, such Amazon Web services cloud computing certification.

My career started in the Navy, and I did 13 years as a Communications Electronics Technician. 5 years of that was in a leadership
position. I gained a certificate IV in Communications electronics as well as a cert IV in leadership and management. Since I left the
Navy I have been working in the resources industry working with automation technology. At the moment I’m working as an automation
specialist on a project to automate all the haulage trucks at an Iron ore mine.

So to get to my dream job, the obvious thing that I am going to need is a bachelor’s degree. But on top of that I am also going to
need significant leadership experience in the technology field. I have already got some experience in leadership, although my current
role lacks that. However my current role is project experience, and the skill that I have learned in this project can definitely help
with this role. I believe that perhaps 5 years of post-graduate experience in a leadership role will be enough to secure a role such a this.
