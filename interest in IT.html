Interest in IT



My Interest in IT relates mostly to my Career. I’m a Truck specialist on an AHS upgrade project in the Pilbara region.
My interest in IT also started through work. I began my Career doing an Electronics trade in the Navy. Back then the work was all
about hardware replacement, however as the fleet upgraded, our systems became IP network based, and were run through software, so I
had to learn a lot about IT. After leaving Defence a couple of years ago I was lucky enough to gain employment in resources, have found
that IT skills are in very high demand.

When I decided to go for the degree in IT, RMIT seemed like the smart choice. Unfortunately my work commitments stop me from attending
classes full time. However, since, almost every university these days offers distance education, it freed me up to choose whatever school
in Australia I wanted. So I did some research, asked some of my smarter friends and the name that kept coming up in regards to technology
courses was RMIT. So I enrolled in this unit.

On viewing the unit list for this degree, I think that it’s quite a broad education in IT. I think there’s going to be a strong focus on
programming and coding in the first year, which I’m looking forward to. I think the nuts and bolts, ‘this does that’ base will serve me
well towards the end, when dealing higher concepts. There won’t be a huge leap in logic for my brain when dealing with things like IT
networking, Software engineering and Web Programming (1)
